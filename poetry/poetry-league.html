League

October 24, 2022 -- 8:18 AM

Of heroes old and new
There is but one to brave the most
A prophesied young newb
To rise and claim the holy boast

It's he who's dexterous
And skilled in sleight of hand
To type so fast and furious
The E's and Q's and comments banned

It's he who's boisterous
And laced with silver tongues and cries
Initiate retreat
The loudest sayings of the wise

Unlikely swordmaster
His blade too sharply for this world
Unlikely maidenful
Two crooked spines refuse to curl

His hand of nimble knives
His tongue of waving, wagging flags
The greatest man alive
If men were to be soggy sacks

Yet nonetheless he struts
He prances to the meadow plains
Then reaches out to God
And all who came and such have failed

“You'll win for us, o child
You'll touch for us, the touchless folk”
Proclaimed the voice of all
The gamers, avid and the broke

Enamored light besieges
The garden lawn below him then
And reaches out to grass
So weeps all of his fellow men

From then, the echoes tread
The minds of every lad and lass
The fingers of this scum
Declared that name shall rightly pass
