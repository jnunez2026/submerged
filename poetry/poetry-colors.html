Learning Colors

October 29, 2022 -- 1:52 AM

Red is the color of sweet apple pie
That grandma serves with a red lip smile
So is the color of the cuts and scrapes
In the hole of my heart where you escaped

Yellow is the color of the radiant sun
Watch it shine so bright in the morning run
Run away from her jaundiced love
Sickened and deficient from some rain above

Orange is the color of autumn leaves
Falling so dearly from mellow trees
Unlike it, meanwhile, is tangerine
Like which my skin is peeled with sourness spread within

Blue is the color of the deep blue sea
Up to the clarity of the sky-high breeze
Down to the pits of my aching soul
Dead inside without you, like a ghoul
