Group of scholars frighten beloved teacher, Sir JM, in explosive ambush

October 24, 2022 -- 10:28 AM

At around 9:40 AM this morning, rhythmic hissing scattered around the Academic Building 3 as a group of scholars organized an ambush against beloved Computer Science teacher, Sir Jan Michael Ibanez.

Thankfully, Sir JM managed to not sustain any injuries, the angel that he is.

However, the deadline to the 1st Quarter Magazine Website Project was still set back to November 2, 2022 due to shock.

Ma'am Charisse Mendoza, a fellow teacher, deducted that the scholars might have gathered under the table from the allure of the donuts mysteriously placed there by an unknown donor.

Both teachers secured their own donuts while most of it got ravaged to be used as a distraction for their escape.

Experts advised teachers to be wary of any future attacks from these gremlins.






P.S. Seriously though, happy birthday po Sir JM! Your classes are always fun po and very much worth the trek from one building to another. I'll be maximizing this deadline extension po so I can write even more nonsensical articles. We all wish you many more years of fulfillment as a teacher and as a wonderful person in general. Thank you po!
